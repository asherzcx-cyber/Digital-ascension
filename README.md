# Digital Ascension

---

## Overview

This project explores **digital ascension** — a theoretical framework for achieving digital immortality while preserving human identity and expanding human ability beyond AI.

It evaluates existing approaches to mind uploading and proposes a new method: the **building-on-top approach**, where the additional artificial neural systems integrate with the biological brain over time.

---

## Key Ideas

* **Digital Immortality:** Preserving identity through computational neuroscience
* **Digital Ascension:** Expanding human cognition beyond biology
* **Pasfurconsistency:** Identity must remain continuous over time (A word invented by me)
* **Sicocortex:** An artificial neural layer that grows alongside the brain

---

### My Proposed Rules

* **First Rule:** Identity continuity must be preserved, which means the transformation must be continuous
* **Second Rule:** Neural changes must not exceed natural brain adaptation rates

---

## Approaches Compared in my first paper, 'A Blueprint for Digital Ascension.'

1. Behavior-based reconstruction → not true identity
2. Brain scanning → breaks continuity
3. Instant replacement → questionable identity
4. Gradual replacement → preserves continuity
5. **Building-on-top (proposed)** → preserves identity + enables enhancement
